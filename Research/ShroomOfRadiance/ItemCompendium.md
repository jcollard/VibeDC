## Treasure & Currency

### Gold
**Sprite:** N/A (coin icon)
**Standard Currency:** Used for all purchases
**Carried Amount:** No weight (abstracted)
**Typical Earnings:**
- Early game (Quests 1-2): 200-400 gold total
- Mid game (Quests 3-4): 600-1000 gold total
- Late game (Quest 5): 1000-1500 gold total
- Endgame (Quest 6-7): 500-1000 gold total
**Total Available:** ~3000-4000 gold across entire game

### Recommended Spending Plan
- **Early Game (200-400g):** Basic equipment upgrades (chain mail, weapons, healing potions)
- **Mid Game (600-1000g):** **Polished Mushroom Cap Shield (500g)**, **Cleansing Flame Gem (400g)**, antifungals
- **Late Game (1000-1500g):** **Protection from Good scroll (250g)**, Fire Essences (300-450g), major healing potions (300-500g), plate mail (400g)
- **Reserve (500g+):** Restoration fund (in case of level drain - 300-500g per character)

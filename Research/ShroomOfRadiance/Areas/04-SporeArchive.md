# Quest 4: The Spore Archive

## Overview
The Spore Archive is an ancient library where knowledge is stored not in books, but in living mushrooms. Each fungal "tome" contains racial memories of Phlegm's history. The Archive holds secrets about the Guardian Morel and the Spore Pool—but it's now infested with petrifying basilisk-shrooms and corrupted scholars.

**Level Range:** 5-6
**Estimated Time:** 20-25 minutes
**Difficulty:** Hard
**Theme:** Knowledge & Danger

---

## Visual Design

### Environment
- **Walls:** Palace walls (biomes 84-87) - ornate, ancient
- **Floors:** Patterned grey (biomes 92) - geometric designs
- **Props:** Large mushrooms (vegetables-6 as "books"), stools/podiums (reading stations), gems (knowledge crystals)
- **Doors:** Palace door (biomes 97)
- **Lighting:** Soft multicolored glow from "knowledge mushrooms"
- **Color Palette:** Rich purples, golds, deep blues - mystical and scholarly

---

## Enemies

### Basilisk-Shroom
**Sprite:** monsters-60 (Beholder - petrifying mushroom)
**Level:** 6
**HP:** 28 (5d8+10)
**AC:** 4
**Attacks:**
- Spore Gaze (2d6 damage + DC 14 CON save or petrified for 1 round)
- Tendril Lash (1d8 damage, melee)

**Special:** **Petrifying Gaze** - Auto-hit ranged attack, save or stunned (petrified)
**Counter:** **Polished Mushroom Cap Shield** - Reflects gaze back (basilisk must save or self-petrify)
**Lore:** *"A grotesque fusion of beholder and mushroom. Its many eyes drip with petrifying spores."*

---

### Mold Guardian
**Sprite:** monsters2-52 (Squib monster)
**Level:** 5
**HP:** 22 (4d8+8)
**AC:** 5
**Attacks:** Fungal Slam (1d10+2 damage)
**Special:** **Spore Cloud** - When hit, releases defensive spores (1d4 damage to attacker)
**Lore:** *"Animated fungal sentinels created to protect the Archive."*

---

### Archive Sentinel (Corrupted Scholar)
**Sprite:** monsters-52 (Humanoid wizard)
**Level:** 6
**HP:** 20 (4d8+4)
**AC:** 6 (robes)
**Attacks:**
- Spore Bolt (2d6+2 magical damage, ranged)
- Stinking Cloud (spell - area disable, DC 13 CON save or incapacitated)

**Special:** **Spellcaster** - Can cast 2-3 spells per combat
**Lore:** *"Once brilliant scholars, now corrupted by the knowledge they sought to protect."*

---

### BOSS: The Head Archivist (Ancient Knowledge Keeper)
**Sprite:** monsters-52 (Humanoid wizard) - LARGE, heavily corrupted
**Level:** 7
**HP:** 50 (7d8+14)
**AC:** 3 (magical wards)
**Attacks:**
- Knowledge Blast (3d6 psychic damage, ignores armor)
- Summon Archive Guardians (2 Mold Guardians, once per fight)
- Petrifying Wave (All enemies in 20 ft cone, DC 15 CON save or petrified for 2 rounds)

**Special:**
- **Phase 1 (50-26 HP):** Ranged spellcasting, summons guards at 35 HP
- **Phase 2 (25-0 HP):** Desperate, uses Petrifying Wave, Knowledge Blasts rapid-fire

**Victory:** Drops **Codex of the Guardian** (lore item) + **Cordyceps of Vitality** (red gem, permanent +1 CON)

---

## Key Items

### The Codex of the Guardian (Quest Item)
**Description:** *"An ancient mushroom tome that pulsates with stored memories. It contains the complete history of the Guardian Morel and the Spore Pool."*
**Function:** Reveals to Sage Morel:
- The Guardian Morel was a benevolent protector
- The Blight is an external parasitic entity
- It possessed the Guardian, not corrupted it
- The Guardian's true self is imprisoned inside
- Destroying The Blight will free the Guardian (but may kill it)

**This is the KEY lore item that sets up the final boss twist.**

---

### Cordyceps of Vitality (Best Item in Game)
**Sprite:** gemwright-15 (Red gem)
**Effect:** Permanent +1 Constitution
- Retroactively increases max HP
- Improves CON saving throws
- Can only be used once per character
**Description:** *"A perfectly preserved cordyceps of pure vitality. Consuming it strengthens your very essence."*

---

### Polished Mushroom Cap Shield (Required Item)
**Sprite:** vegetables-6 (Big mushroom, held as shield)
**Effect:** Reflects basilisk-shroom gaze attacks
**Cost:** 500 gold (must buy from Sporecery before entering)
**Description:** *"A polished mushroom cap shield. Its reflective surface can deflect petrifying gazes."*
**Note:** REQUIRED for survival. Game should hint at this.

---

## Quest Structure

### Objective 1: Acquire Polished Mushroom Cap Shield
**Description:** *"Amanita warns you that the Spore Archive is guarded by basilisk-shrooms. You'll need protection."*
**Task:** Buy shield from Sporecery (500 gold)
**Hint:** NPC dialogue heavily suggests this is REQUIRED

---

### Objective 2: Navigate the Archive
**Description:** *"Explore the Spore Archive and find knowledge about the Guardian Morel."*
**Task:** Fight through 4-5 encounters, reach Inner Sanctum

---

### Objective 3: Defeat the Head Archivist
**Description:** *"The corrupted Head Archivist guards the deepest knowledge. Defeat him to access the Codex."*
**Task:** Boss fight
**Reward:** Cordyceps of Vitality, Codex of the Guardian

---

### Objective 4: Return with the Codex
**Description:** *"Bring the Codex of the Guardian to Sage Morel."*
**Task:** Return to New Phlegm
**Reward:** 500 gold, Copper Key, Quest 5 unlocked, Protection from Good scroll (discounted)

---

## Encounters

### Encounter 1: Reading Room Ambush
**Enemies:** 2 Basilisk-Shrooms, 2 Mold Guardians
**Difficulty:** Hard (if no shield), Medium (with shield)
**Purpose:** Punish players who ignored the shield warning
**Tactic:** Use shield to reflect gazes, focus fire

---

### Encounter 2: Scholar's Hall
**Enemies:** 3 Archive Sentinels (wizards)
**Difficulty:** Medium-Hard
**Purpose:** Test anti-caster tactics (interrupt spells)
**Tactic:** Rush casters, prevent Stinking Cloud casts

---

### Encounter 3: The Gauntlet
**Enemies:** 3 Basilisk-Shrooms, 3 Mold Guardians
**Difficulty:** Very Hard
**Purpose:** Resource drain before boss
**Tactic:** Careful positioning, shield use, focus fire

---

### Encounter 4: Inner Sanctum (BOSS)
**Enemy:** Head Archivist
**Difficulty:** Very Hard
**Phases:** 2
**Special:** Summons adds, petrifying wave
**Tactic:** Kill adds fast, spread out, save burst for Phase 2

---

## Events & Story Beats

### Arrival
*"You enter a vast hall of impossible geometry. Mushrooms tower like bookshelves, their caps glowing with stored knowledge. The air hums with psychic energy. This is a place of power—and it has been violated."*

---

### The Codex Revelation
**Trigger:** Defeat Head Archivist, examine Codex
**Text:** Sage Morel (when returned): *"This changes everything. The Guardian Morel is not evil—it's a prisoner! The Blight wears its face like a mask. If we can destroy The Blight, we may free the Guardian... but I fear it may cost the Guardian its life. Such is the tragedy of possession."*
**Impact:** Sets up final boss emotional stakes
